Alpha Cal uses advanced AI algorithms to monitor all major platforms including Twitter, Discord, Telegram, and launchpads in real-time. Our system analyzes social sentiment, contract deployments, and community activity to instantly detect and alert you about new launches.

What makes Alpha Cal different from other launch trackers?

Alpha Cal combines AI-powered sentiment analysis, rug risk detection, and premium whitelist access in one platform. We don't just track launches - we analyze them for safety and potential, giving you the insights you need to make informed trading decisions.

The $ACAL token is the utility token that powers the Alpha Cal ecosystem. Holders can stake tokens to unlock premium features, guaranteed whitelist access, priority support, and exclusive trading insights. Higher tiers provide more benefits and early access to high-potential launches.
